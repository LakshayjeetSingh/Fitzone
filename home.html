ABOUT FITZONE

Fitzone is a dream, A vision to make everybody fit.

Fitness is the state of being healthy.The ability to carry out everyday activities without becoming excessively fatigued,and to still have enough energy left to deal with emergencies.

Fitness is of two types :

Physical fitness and Mental fitness ,

Physical fitness has 5 components:-

Muscular Strength, Muscular Endurance, Cardiovascular Endurance, Flexibility, Body fat composition,

Mental Fitness has following components

Coordination in body, Non-verbal communication, Emotional balance, Response Flexibility, Fear modulation, Insights, Empathy, Intuition, Morality,
